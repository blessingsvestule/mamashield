🔔 Set your vaccine reminder with SOUND

A silent notification is easy to miss. Follow these steps to make sure your phone makes a sound — even on silent mode.

⚠️ Important: There is a difference between a Google Calendar notification (silent banner) and an alarm (makes sound). You need the ALARM setting so your phone rings even if it is on silent or Do Not Disturb.
1
After tapping "+ Add to Calendar", Google Calendar opens with the event already filled in
2
Scroll down on the event page to find "Add notification"
3
Tap the dropdown that says "Notification" and change it to "Alarm" — this makes a sound
4
Set the time — choose "1 week before" for your first reminder. Tap "Add notification" again and add "1 day before" as a second reminder.
5
Tap Save ✅ — your phone will now ring with an alarm before every vaccine appointment
6
Also go to your phone Settings → Apps → Google Calendar → Notifications and make sure notifications are turned ON and set to "High importance"
1
After tapping "+ Add to Calendar", Google Calendar opens with the event filled in
2
Tap "Add alert" on the event page
3
Select "1 week before" — then tap "Add another alert" and select "1 day before"
4
Tap Save — the alerts will appear as iPhone notifications with sound
5
To ensure sound: go to iPhone Settings → Notifications → Google Calendar → turn on Allow Notifications and set Sounds to ON
6
⚠️ iPhone tip: Calendar alerts on iPhone play sound even on silent mode — but only if you go to Settings → Sounds → Calendar Alerts and select a ringtone
